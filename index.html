Got Questions?

Frequently asked questions.

How do I book pet sitting services with Pets and The City? +
Easy! Just call or text Samantha at 701-509-3856. We'll set up a free meet and greet so your pet can get comfortable before the first visit.
What areas of Kansas City do you serve? +
We cover a wide range of Kansas City, MO neighborhoods including Westport, Plaza, Brookside, and surrounding areas. Reach out to confirm availability in your specific area.
What pets do you care for? +
We primarily care for dogs and cats, but we're happy to discuss other small animals too. Just give us a call and we'll figure it out together.
What does an overnight pet sitting stay include? +
Overnight stays mean Samantha sleeps at your home. Your pet stays in their own space, follows their normal routine, and gets full care including feeding, walks, playtime, and photo updates for you.
Can I get updates while I'm away? +
Yes! Photo updates and check-ins come with all our services so you can relax knowing your pet is happy and well cared for.
What makes Pets and The City different from other pet sitters in Kansas City? +
We offer personal, one-on-one care with clear communication. Samantha knows Kansas City neighborhoods and sends photo updates with every visit. We always do a meet and greet first, and we stick to your pet's routine.
How much does pet sitting cost in Kansas City? +
Pricing depends on the service and how long you need care. Call Samantha at 701-509-3856 for a quote based on what you need and where you are in Kansas City.
